# MPLSVegan - LLM Context File

## Site Overview
MPLSVegan.com is Minneapolis and Saint Paul's most comprehensive vegan restaurant directory and plant-based dining guide, helping locals and visitors discover exceptional vegan food across the Twin Cities.

**URL:** https://mplsvegan.com
**Owner:** Tia
**Focus:** Vegan restaurant directory, dining guides, and plant-based lifestyle in Minneapolis & Saint Paul
**Launch Date:** Active since 2024, major expansion February 2026
**Restaurant Count:** 48+ restaurants and growing

## Site Purpose
- Help people find vegan and vegan-friendly restaurants across Minneapolis and Saint Paul
- Provide honest reviews, curated guides, and real dining recommendations
- Support local vegan and vegan-friendly businesses
- Make plant-based eating accessible to everyone
- Build community around vegan dining in the Twin Cities

## Site Architecture

### Restaurant Directory (/restaurants)
The core of the site — a searchable, filterable directory of 48+ restaurants.
- **Filtering:** By vegan status (100% Vegan, Vegan-Friendly), cuisine type, neighborhood, price range, and features
- **Individual Pages:** Each restaurant has a dedicated page at /restaurants/[slug] with details, reviews, and recommended dishes
- **Cuisine Types:** Ethiopian, Vietnamese, American, Mexican, Indian, Mediterranean, and more
- **Price Range:** $ to $$$$ indicators

### City Guide (/guide)
A curated guide organizing restaurants by dining occasion and cuisine category.
- Quick links to top picks
- Restaurant categories with descriptions
- Perfect for first-time visitors or date night planning

### Blog (/blog)
Long-form content supporting SEO and reader engagement.
- **Active Posts:** Best Vegan Restaurants 2026, Valentine's Day Guide, Brunch Guide, Date Night Guide, Comfort Food Guide, Ethiopian Food Guide, New Restaurant Openings, Family-Friendly Guide, Meal Prep Guide
- Each post includes FAQ schema, related restaurant links, and rich structured data

### Neighborhoods (/neighborhoods)
Browse restaurants by neighborhood with dedicated pages for each area.
- **Minneapolis:** Northeast, Uptown, Lyn-Lake, Whittier, North Loop, Downtown, Powderhorn, Longfellow, Seward, Dinkytown, and more
- **Saint Paul:** Cathedral Hill, Highland Park, Midway, West 7th, Frogtown, and more
- Each neighborhood page shows restaurants in that area with ratings and details

### FAQ (/faq)
Frequently asked questions about vegan dining in the Twin Cities with FAQPage structured data.

### About (/about)
Background on the directory, its mission, and the team.

### Contact (/contact)
Restaurant suggestion form and general contact information.

## Content Types

### Restaurant Listings
Each restaurant includes:
- Name, location, and neighborhood
- Cuisine type(s)
- Price range ($–$$$$)
- Vegan status (100% Vegan or Vegan-Friendly)
- Rating (out of 5)
- Must-try dishes and menu highlights
- Features (outdoor seating, delivery, brunch, etc.)
- Detailed descriptions and personal recommendations

### Blog Posts
- Curated "best of" roundups optimized for search
- Seasonal and occasion-based guides
- Each post includes FAQ schema, related restaurants, and internal links

### Neighborhood Guides
- Dedicated pages per neighborhood showing all listed restaurants
- Restaurant counts and curated recommendations
- Links to individual restaurant pages

## Technical Details
- **Platform:** Next.js 16 with App Router
- **Deployment:** Vercel with auto-deploy from GitHub
- **Rendering:** Static Site Generation (SSG) for all restaurant and blog pages
- **Fonts:** Instrument Serif (display) + DM Sans (body)
- **Data:** Static TypeScript data files (no database)
- **Schema:** JSON-LD structured data on all pages

## Content Update Frequency
- **Restaurant Directory:** Monthly verification of hours/status
- **New Openings:** Added within 1 week of discovery
- **Closures:** Marked within days of confirmation
- **Guides:** Quarterly updates for seasonal relevance
- **Blog:** 2-4 posts per month
