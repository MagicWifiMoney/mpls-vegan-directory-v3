# MPLSVegan - LLM Context File

## Site Overview
MPLSVegan.com is Minneapolis' comprehensive vegan restaurant and dining guide, helping locals and visitors discover plant-based food options across the Twin Cities.

**URL:** https://mplsvegan.com
**Owner:** Tia
**Focus:** Vegan dining, restaurants, recipes, and plant-based lifestyle in Minneapolis/St. Paul
**Launch Date:** Active since 2024, major expansion February 2026

## Site Purpose
- Help people find vegan-friendly restaurants in Minneapolis
- Provide honest reviews and dining recommendations
- Support local vegan and vegan-friendly businesses
- Make plant-based eating accessible to everyone
- Build community around vegan dining in the Twin Cities
